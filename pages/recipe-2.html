Spicy Mango Chicken

Experience a delightful explosion of flavors with our Spicy Mango Chicken recipe. This dish
combines the sweetness of ripe mangoes with the bold kick of spices, creating a mouthwatering
harmony that will leave you craving more. Tender chicken pieces marinated and cooked to
perfection, this recipe is a celebration of vibrant ingredients and exotic tastes.

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1 red chili, finely chopped (adjust according to spice preference)
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a bowl, combine the chicken cubes with half of the chopped garlic, grated ginger, curry
    powder, turmeric powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well to coat the chicken evenly. Let it marinate for
    at least 30 minutes in the refrigerator.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the remaining garlic and finely chopped red chili.
    Sauté until aromatic. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent.

  3. Cook the Chicken: Add the marinated chicken to the pan. Cook until the chicken is browned on all sides and cooked
    through. Remove the chicken from the pan and set it aside.

  4. Prepare the Mango Sauce: In the same pan, add the diced mangoes. Cook until they become soft and slightly caramelized. Using a fork or a masher, mash the mangoes to create a sauce-like consistency.

  5. Combine and Simmer: Return the cooked chicken to the pan with the mango sauce. Stir well to coat the chicken with
    the sauce. Let it simmer for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Adjust seasoning
    with salt and pepper according to taste.

  6. Serve and Garnish: Serve the Spicy Mango Chicken over cooked rice. Garnish with fresh chopped cilantro for a burst
    of freshness and color. Enjoy this spicy, fruity, and savory dish that will take your taste buds on a
    tantalizing journey.

Tips:

  • Adjust Spice Level: Control the spice level by adding or reducing the amount of red chili according
    to your preference. For a milder version, remove the seeds from the chili before chopping.

  • Ripe Mangoes: Use ripe mangoes for the sauce. They should be sweet and juicy to enhance the
    natural sweetness of the dish.

  • Side Suggestions: Pair the Spicy Mango Chicken with naan bread or steamed vegetables for a
    complete and balanced meal.
