Decadent Chocolate Brownies

Indulge your sweet cravings with our Decadent Chocolate Brownies. These moist, fudgy brownies
are the epitome of chocolatey goodness. Baked to perfection and topped with a glossy chocolate
ganache, they are a true delight for chocolate lovers. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion
or simply treating yourself, these brownies are the ultimate indulgence.

Ingredients for the brownies:

  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups (400g) granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (120g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup (60g) cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup (90g) chocolate chips

For the Chocolate Ganache:

  • 1/2 cup (120ml) heavy cream
  • 1 cup (180g) semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat and Prepare: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line a 9x9-inch (23x23cm) baking pan with
    parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal.

  2. Mix the Batter: In a large mixing bowl, combine the melted butter and sugar. Add the eggs and vanilla extract,
    and whisk until well combined. In a separate bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, and salt.
    Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Fold in the
    chocolate chips.

  3. Bake the Brownies: Pour the brownie batter into the prepared pan, spreading it evenly. Bake in the preheated oven
    for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist
    crumbs. Be careful not to overbake.

  4. Make the Chocolate Ganache: While the brownies are cooling, prepare the chocolate ganache. In a small saucepan, heat the
    heavy cream over medium heat until it starts to simmer. Remove from heat and add the
    chocolate chips and butter. Let it sit for a minute to melt, then whisk until smooth and glossy.

  5. Frost the Brownies: Once the brownies have completely cooled, pour the chocolate ganache over the top, spreading
    it evenly with a spatula. Allow the ganache to set for a few hours at room temperature or in the refrigerator.

  6. Slice and Enjoy: Use the parchment paper overhangs to lift the brownies out of the pan. Place on a cutting board
    and, using a sharp knife, cut into squares. Serve and enjoy these decadent chocolate brownies
    with your favorite hot beverage or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Tips:

  • Quality Chocolate: Use high-quality chocolate for both the brownies and the ganache to enhance
    the rich chocolate flavor.

  • Cooling Time: Allow the brownies to cool completely before cutting to achieve clean, neat slices.

  • Storage: Store leftover brownies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days, or
    refrigerate for longer shelf life.
